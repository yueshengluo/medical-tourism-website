About Chengdu Medical Tourism

Bridging continents for exceptional healthcare experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Chengdu Medical Tourism emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make world-class healthcare accessible and affordable for seniors and patients across North America and Europe.

Our founders, healthcare professionals with decades of experience in both Western and Chinese medical systems, recognized the extraordinary quality of care available in Chengdu's premier medical facilities. Combined with significant cost savings and the rich cultural experience of Sichuan province, we saw an opportunity to transform healthcare journeys.

Today, we've successfully facilitated treatments for over 500 international patients, maintaining a 98% satisfaction rate and building lasting relationships between our clients and Chengdu's medical community.

Why Chengdu?

The perfect destination for medical tourism

Medical Excellence Hub

Chengdu has emerged as Southwest China's premier medical destination, home to some of the country's most advanced hospitals and research institutions. The city's medical facilities rival those found anywhere in the world, with cutting-edge technology and internationally trained specialists.

Rich Cultural Experience

Beyond medical care, Chengdu offers a unique cultural immersion. Known for its laid-back lifestyle, incredible cuisine, and as the home of giant pandas, the city provides a peaceful, healing environment perfect for recovery.

Traditional Medicine Integration

Chengdu is a center for Traditional Chinese Medicine, allowing us to offer holistic treatment approaches that combine modern medical procedures with ancient healing practices for optimal recovery.
